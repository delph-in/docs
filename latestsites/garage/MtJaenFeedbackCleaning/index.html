    Feedback Cleaning of JaEn MTRs on the Tanaka Corpus

    Feedback cleaning of JaEn MTRs in the spirit of Imamura et. al (ACL 2003). Calculate the average of the delta between the best translation for each sentence covered by a rule and the translations not including that rule. We will throw out rules that have a delta worse than a certain thereshold while not affecting too many unambiguous translations.
