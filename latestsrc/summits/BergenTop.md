{% raw %}# Overview

The 22nd DELPH-IN [Summit](https://delph-in.github.io/docs/summits/SummitTop) will take place over July 27-31 2026, at [Western Norway University of Applied Sciences](https://hvl.no/en), in Bergen, Norway. ## Venue

The first three plenary days will happen in **Room C225**, on the **2nd floor (Section C)** of Western Norway University of Applied Sciences' **Main Building** [(Google link)](https://maps.app.goo.gl/rBqzCGeozDrWkkqg7). Once inside the building, take the big stairs one floor up, and then walk until you find the room. Or you can use [MazeMap](https://use.mazemap.com/#v=1&campusid=9&zlevel=1&center=5.350563,60.368798&zoom=18.4&desttype=identifier&dest=Kronstad%201-C225&starttype=identifier&start=Kronstad%201-A101).

Someone will be at the main entrance to open the door for you at the beginning of day one (Monday 27). If you arrive later, contact Petter Haugereid ([phau@hvl.no](mailto:phau@hvl.no)), and he will come and open the door for you.

# Travel & Accommodation

## From the airport

From Bergen Airport the light rail called 'Bybanen' goes to the city centre. Bybanen stops at the campus on Kronstad. (The stop is called Kronstad.) A ticket with Bybanen costs approximately 50 NOK (4.50 euros) and takes approximately 30 minutes to Kronstad and 45 minutes to the city centre. Bybanen is the best and cheapest alternative if you are arriving by plane. Tickets can be bought at every Bybanen-stop, and the card used to pay functions as the ticket. Notice that Bybanen will be partly closed for maintenance until July 26. If you arrive on Sunday the 26th, you can take Bybanen from the airport, but you will be transferred to a bus on the way to the city centre. From July 27, Bybanen will run as usual.

There are airport buses running between the airport and the city centre. These do not stop at the campus. A ticket with the airport bus costs approximately 150 NOK.

A taxi from Bergen Airport Flesland takes approximately 30 minutes to the campus at Kronstad, depending on traffic.

## Getting to the campus from the city centre

If you arrive by train or boat, you will need to go to the campus from the city centre. The easiest way to get to Campus Kronstad, is to take the light rail, 'Bybanen.' The campus has a stop with the same name. Both Bybanen-lines stop at campus Kronstad.
