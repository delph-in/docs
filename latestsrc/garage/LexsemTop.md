{% raw %}This page describes ongoing work to incorporate more lexical semantics
into DELPH-IN.

Prelimary work is being done using the ERG and wordnet, mainly at NTU
(FrancisBond, [MathieuMorey](/MathieuMorey)) and UW
(ZinaPozen).
